The Best Movies According to Justin

My top 3 movies of all-time.(In no particular order)

The Unbearable Weight of Massive Talent

This movie about movies has a funny script and great actors. The chemistry between Nicholas Cage and Pedro Pascal is just fantastic.

Donnie Darko

Everytime I think I understand this movie and then rewatch it there's something else I don't understand. That being said it's my favourite movie with time travel.

The Thing (1982)

A classic movie for a reason it's filled with suspense and paranoia, blending practical effects with intense psychological horror.
